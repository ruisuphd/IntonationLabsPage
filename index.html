About

Meet the founder.

Rui Su — Founder & Director

AI Engineer, AI Consultant, and PhD candidate at Maynooth University (Ireland) specialising in ensemble prediction, statistical modelling, and low-latency algorithm design. Bilingual in English and Mandarin Chinese, based between Singapore and Dublin.

Before founding Intonation Labs, Rui built Instant Harmonies — a production-deployed system for real-time music analysis — published 3 peer-reviewed papers on real-time audio ML, and earned 6 IBM Professional Certificates across data science, AI engineering, and generative AI.

Intonation Labs sits at a rare intersection: deep academic research, production engineering experience, and hands-on GenAI expertise. We don't just advise — we build.
